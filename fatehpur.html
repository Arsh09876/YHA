Fatehpur Sikri

History

Fatehpur Sikri was founded in 1571 by Mughal Emperor Akbar and served as the capital of the Mughal Empire for nearly 14 years. Built in red sandstone, the city reflects a unique blend of Persian, Islamic, and Indian architectural styles and is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
